Pilestredet 32

N002.107

The Big Auditorium

N002.107, fondly known as the Big Auditorium, is a hallmark of academic grandeur located within the Pilestredet 32 building. With a substantial room capacity accommodating up to 440 students, it stands as a testament to OsloMet's commitment to fostering large-scale learning experiences. The auditorium boasts a stylish design, featuring seats adorned in the university's vibrant yellow and orange palette, symbolizing the institution's spirit of innovation and creativity. What sets this auditorium apart is its thoughtful layout, which includes a second-floor level, ensuring that extra students can comfortably participate in lectures and events.

N010.023

The Small Auditorium

N010.023, affectionately referred to as the Small Auditorium, exudes a sense of cozy charm within the Pilestredet 32 building. Despite its quaint appearance, this auditorium plays a vital role in OsloMet's academic landscape, accommodating up to 91 students. The seats, adorned in a warm beige/brown hue, are complemented by distinctive red railings and red floors, adding a touch of character to the space. While its design might be considered old-fashioned, the Small Auditorium more than makes up for it with its functionality and intimate atmosphere.

Library

Health Sciences

Situated on the 3rd floor, P32 houses the Health Sciences Library, also known as Helsefagbiblioteket. This library caters specifically to students, faculty, and researchers in health-related fields. Here’s what you’ll find:

  • Health Sciences Resources: The library offers an extensive collection of resources related to medicine, nursing, public health, and other health disciplines. Whether you’re studying anatomy, pharmacology, or epidemiology, you’ll find relevant materials here.
  • Study Spaces: Need a quiet spot to study? P32 provides study areas equipped with comfortable seating, tables, and access to power outlets.
  • Expert Assistance: Librarians and subject specialists are available to assist with research, literature searches, and navigating databases.
  • E-Books and Journals: Access digital resources, including e-books, medical journals, and scholarly articles.
  • Health Sciences Community:P32 Library serves as a hub for collaboration, discussions, and knowledge exchange among health science enthusiasts.

Andrea Spiseri

P32 Cafeteria

Situated on the 1st floor, P32 houses Kafé Andrea - a cafeteria where you can indulge in a variety of offerings:

  • Hot Meals: Help yourself to hot dishes, soups, and a selection of toppings and bread from our buffet. Payment is based on weight.
  • Wraps and Spreads: Explore a wide range of wraps and freshly prepared spreads.
  • Irresistible Pastries: Treat yourself to tempting cakes and baked goods.
  • Quality Coffee: Enjoy a variety of expertly brewed coffee drinks.

All this is served in spacious, well-lit, and modern surroundings. You may sit and enjoy your meal in a spacious seating area accomodating up to 500 students.
