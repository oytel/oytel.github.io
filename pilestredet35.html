Pilestredet 35

This building accommodates the social sciences, technology, art & design faculties, 8 seminar rooms, 12 classrooms, 10 auditoriums, 29 computer labs, 29 group rooms, 2 quiet rooms, and a student commons with lounge areas and kitchen facilities.

PH170

Gerd Hagens Auditorium

PH170, known as Gerd Hagens, is an auditorium situated on the first floor of P35. Named in her honour in 2018, it is a tribute to the pioneering work of Gerd Hagen in Norwegian child welfare. This auditorium accommodates 350 students in its half-circle arrangement of navy blue chairs. Its focal point is a large screen, empowering professors to deliver engaging lectures and interactive sessions. This space marries legacy and innovation, embodying OsloMet's commitment to exceptional education and collaborative learning.

PH131

Anna Felbers Auditorium

Nestled on the first floor of Building Pilestredet 35, Anna Felbers auditorium pays homage to Anna Felber, a distinguished Norwegian social worker and politician associated with the Norwegian Labour Party. This auditorium offers an intimate setting for academic engagements with a seating capacity of 136 students. Embracing the same navy blue color palette as its counterpart, Gerd Hages auditorium, Anna Felbers auditorium distinguishes itself with a traditional straight-row seating arrangement. Despite its smaller size, the auditorium exudes an atmosphere of focused learning, reflecting the legacy of Anna Felber's dedication to education and social welfare.

Library

Multifaceted Resource Center

Nestled on the 3rd floor of P35, the P35 Library caters to a wide range of academic interests. Here’s what you’ll find:

  • Plethora of Resources: Whether you’re studying engineering, business, social work, or computer science, our library houses a wealth of materials to fuel your intellectual curiosity.
  • Study Spaces: There are group study rooms available on the third floor of both P35 and PP35. You can reserve a study room in P35 through the student platform.
  • Expert Assistance: Librarians and subject specialists are available to assist with research, literature searches, and navigating databases.
  • Community of Curiostiy: Beyond books, the P35 Library fosters connections. Engage in discussions, collaborate with peers, and ignite your passion for knowledge.

Olaf Spiseri

P35 Cafeteria

Situated on the 1st floor, P35 houses Kafé Olaf - a cafeteria where you can indulge in a variety of offerings:

  • Hot Meals: Help yourself to hot dishes, soups, and a selection of toppings and bread from our buffet. Payment is based on weight.
  • Wraps and Spreads: Explore a wide range of wraps and freshly prepared spreads.
  • Irresistible Pastries: Treat yourself to tempting cakes and baked goods.
  • Quality Coffee: Enjoy a variety of expertly brewed coffee drinks.

All this is served in spacious, well-lit, and modern surroundings. You may sit and enjoy your meal in a spacious seating area, Olaf Spiseri, accomodating up to 500 students.
